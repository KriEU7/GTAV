Michael De Santa





Michael De Santa is one of the three main characters of Gta5. Born in 1965, probably in the
Middle West, which would explain why his criminal career took place mainly between North Yankton
and Carcer City. Raised in a poor and degraded environment, he had an alcoholic father who often
abused him. He started playing American football on his high school team. He showed great talent
as a player, appearing in several local newspapers. However, due to his behavioral problems and
continuing injuries, he was forced to retire. In 1985, at the age of 20, he made his first bank
robbery in the suburbs of Carcer City, managing to steal $10,000.

In the same year he was arrested twice. In addition to the robberies,
Michael was also a drug smuggler. Despite several arrests,
Michael has become one of America’s most wanted criminals.
A few years later he met Trevor Philips and Brad Snider,
with whom he formed a team of successful robbers.


Despite the excellent friendship between Michael and Trevor, the latter did not get along with Amanda,
Michael’s future wife. Together they have two children namely Tracey and Jimmy.

Michael then decided to become more cautious as a criminal,
causing disagreements with Trevor. In 2004, Michael, Trevor, and Brad organized a robbery at the offices
of a security transport company in Ludendorff (North Yankton), which had a disastrous outcome.
Pursued by dozens of cops, the three head for the escape point, where they find an ambush by other cops;
Brad is killed and Michael is wounded, forcing Trevor to escape. Michael decides to make a deal with FIB
agent Dave Northon, according to which Michael is declared dead (Brad is buried instead) and Dave receives
credit for his killing.
