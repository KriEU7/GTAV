Trevor Philips





Trevor Philips is one of the three main characters in GTA V. He was born and
raised in Canada by an abusive father and a mother who was indifferent to
him. He later discovers that he is a good aviation pilot, but two days before
completing his training he is discharged because of his mental stability. He
later turns to the world of crime, but on his first robbery he is arrested and
taken to prison.

After getting out of prison, Trevor met Micheal de Santa (another GTA V
protagonist) and together they formed a criminal group. Later Trevor met Brad
Snider, who also joined this criminal gang. In 2004 they organized
a bank robbery in Lundendorff, after robbing it according to their plan they
would manage to get on a helicopter and escape, but actually not as hoped. In
fact, they were surrounded by police, Micheal and Snider were hit
by bullets (only Snider died), so Trevor in order not to abandon his friends started to fight
but after a while had to flee. Shortly afterwards there was also confirmation that Micheal was
dead (but that he was not actually dead).
Trevor managed to escape and return to town where he settled in the rural town of Sandy
Shores, where he formed a new gang with Ron Jakowski, Wade Herbert, and Chef.
