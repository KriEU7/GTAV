Franklin Clinton






Franklin Clinton is one of the three main characters in Gta5.
Born in 1988 in South Los Santos, Franklin grew up in an environment
where he could not rely on family, money, or education.
He attended Davis High School along with Lamar Davis and Tonya Wiggins.

Shortly after her birth, her mother developed a crack addiction, which led to her death,
and left her home in Franklin and her aunt Denise. He later joined a gang called The Families,
which included his friend Lamar. Together with him he made small robberies
and dealt drugs but was arrested shortly after;
he then decided to abandon his old life and to get out of the ghetto and was released.
